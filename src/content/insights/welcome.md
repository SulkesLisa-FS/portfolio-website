## Hello, and thanks for stopping by

I'm Lisa, a full-stack developer. I build secure backend architectures and responsive, user-friendly interfaces. The purpose of this site is to document my projects, share technical insights, and track my continuous learning as I develop new applications.

## What you'll find here

- **Projects** — A look at applications I've built, from the initial idea to the live product.
- **About** — My journey through development and the technologies I work with.
- **Insights** — What I'm learning, new discoveries, and reflections from real projects.

## Why I write

We all start somewhere in our journey. I want to share what I learn and discover with others who might be starting out or on a similar path. Whether it's a new framework, a security best practice, or a problem-solving strategy, I want this section to be genuinely useful.
